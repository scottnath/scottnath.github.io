About Scott Nath

tl;dr: Scott Nath is a web developer and actor living in New York State

Web development

I am a Frontend Architect, which also means Solutions Architect, Software Architect, Full Stack Developer, DevOps Architect, Design Engineer, Senior Software Engineer, Design System Architect, Developer Advocate or any other of a long list of new-hotness titles. I’m a polyglot, full-stack engineer who specializes in the front-end - which really just means I care about every User getting what they need.

You can boil down what I do to four things:

  1. I build performant and accessible UIs in whatever flavor frontend you like
  2. I mentor and equip development teams to efficiently do the same
  3. I build DevX that tests, prototypes and deploys
  4. I figure out how to make things work that shouldn’t work
